When you visit our Site, certain information is automatically collected by our web server, content delivery network (Cloudflare), and third-party services including Google AdSense. This data collection is standard across nearly all websites and is necessary for the Site to function properly, deliver content efficiently, and serve advertisements.

We and our third-party partners, including Google AdSense and its advertising partners, use cookies, browser localStorage, web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to operate essential site functions, remember your preferences (such as dark mode, volume, favorite games, and stealth settings), provide analytics about how the Site is used, detect and prevent abuse, and serve and measure advertisements. A complete, up-to-date list of all cookies used on the Site, including their purpose, duration, and whether they are first-party or third-party, is maintained in our separate Cookie Notice accessible via the footer. By continuing to use the Site, you consent to the placement of these cookies in accordance with this policy.
